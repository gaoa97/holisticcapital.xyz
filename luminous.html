🔴Cultural CapitalRitual, story, song. How a field remembers itself.
🟠Experiential CapitalWhat the body knows. Wisdom earned through living.
🟡Financial CapitalMoney in its right place. One capital among eight.
🟢Social CapitalTrust held between people. The current of being known.

🟣Spiritual CapitalThe unnameable centre. The reason any of it is worth tending.

Where Your Contribution Goes

Financial Capital in Service of the Whole

Luminous is the Eight Forms of Capital practised in real time. When you take a Patron or Founding Patron place, your Financial Capital moves in service of the other seven, and of the people who carry them.

Every Patron place gifts a Supported place to someone whose wealth is real but not financial, rich in spiritual, intellectual, living or material capital, with cost the only thing in the way. Your contribution is what puts them in the room. It is how the circle stays whole, composed of who belongs rather than only who can pay.

Ten percent of everything raised at Luminous is set aside for a regenerative cause the Circle chooses together, our first act of collective allocation, voted on by the community. A small beginning, and a deliberate one.

Because this is the Genesis Gathering, a Founding Patron place seeds more than a weekend. It is the first contribution toward a community-governed treasury forming as Holistic Capital takes shape, a pool the Circle will one day steward together, directing capital toward regenerative projects we choose as one. To seed it now is to help decide what this field becomes.

And a Founding Patron holds the door open for everyone else. At the close of Day Two, there is a place to share your vision and the work you carry, witnessed by the circle you helped convene.

Come and feel it.
If by the midday break on the first day you know this is not your path, let a steward know and we will return your contribution in full, with our blessing. There is no risk in saying yes.
