Built on the Eight Forms of Capital

Wealth as Wholeness

Modern life measures wealth one way: money. Holistic Capital recognises eight, woven around relational capital at their centre. Each form is a fractal of the whole, holding all the others within it, and Luminous is built on every one.

Living Soil, water, forest, body: ecosystems coming back to life.
Spiritual The unnameable centre, the reason any of it is worth tending.
Cultural Ritual, story, song: how a field remembers itself.
Material Land, tools, shelter: the made world we move through.
Financial Money in its right place, one capital among eight.
Social Trust held between people. The current of being known.
Experiential What the body knows: wisdom earned through living.
Intellectual Ideas that grow as they are shared.

Wealth is wholeness. Simply remembered.
