How to Become a Member

How One Joins the Field

Holistic Capital is entered by invitation, and the journey begins with a single conversation. Here is the path from first contact to belonging, step by step. The first step is to book an Alignment Call.

1

Alignment Call

The first step. A relaxed conversation with one of the stewards, to feel whether there is genuine resonance, both ways. No pitch and no pressure, simply a meeting. Book yours whenever you feel ready.

Book Your Alignment Call
2

Experience the Field

Join us in person at a gathering such as the Luminous Seeding. This is where you feel the field in your body, and where trust is grown through shared experience.

3

Application & Capital Map

When the resonance is mutual, you map yourself across the eight forms of capital. A deepening of self-knowledge, and a way for the field to see where your gifts belong.

4

Steward Review & Invitation

The stewards reflect on your application and, where there is coherence, extend a personal invitation to join the Circle.

5

Commitment

You affirm the covenant, a shared agreement to place relationship and care at the centre. This is the step where membership is confirmed and the annual membership fee is paid, and where you take your place in the Circle.

6

Integration

Your first ninety days, held by the field. A capital-map conversation, a welcome into the Circle, and a peer pod, so you are known quickly.

7

Belonging & Contribution

You move from guest to contributor, sharing your gifts, so the flourishing of one becomes the flourishing of all.
